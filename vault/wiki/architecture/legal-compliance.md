# Legal / Compliance Boundary

## Product Stance

The project must operate as a prediction/voting service with no money.

Allowed concepts:

- prediction;
- vote;
- support;
- confidence points;
- rating points;
- leaderboard;
- badges;
- public support statistics.

## Red Lines

Do not add without a separate legal decision:

- card/SBP/crypto payments;
- user monetary balance;
- deposits;
- withdrawals;
- odds;
- payouts;
- paid entry;
- organizer commission;
- real-money prizes;
- prizes with property value for prediction results;
- bookmaker-style bet settlement.

## Why This Matters

A money stake or prize-bearing wager can become regulated gambling/bookmaker activity. That would require legal review, licensing/permissions, KYC, age restrictions, tax handling, payment infrastructure, regulator compliance, and a different product architecture.

## Safe Product Language

Prefer:

- prediction;
- prediction;
- vote;
- confidence points;
- rating points;
- support percentage;
- leaderboard.

Avoid in UI/API/product docs:

- bet;
- deposit;
- withdrawal;
- odds;
- win/winnings;
- payout;
- bookmaker;
- cash prize.

## Future Money Model

If money is ever considered, treat it as a separate project. Start with legal counsel and licensed-operator strategy before technical design.
